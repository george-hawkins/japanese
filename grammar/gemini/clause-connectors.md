# JLPT N5 Clause Connectors — Cheat Sheet

A compact reference for the clause-connecting words you need at N5: words that glue sentences together with meanings like *but*, *because*, *when*, *and then*.

Most attach to a **plain-form sentence** ending (e.g. `あつい` + `から`, `行く` + `とき`). とき also accepts polite forms before it.

## Categories at a glance

- **Contrast (but / however):** けど, が, でも
- **Reason / cause (because / so):** から, ので, だから, それで
- **Sequence (and then):** そして, それから
- **Addition (and also):** それに *(slightly above N5, but appears in textbooks)*
- **Time (when):** とき

## The connectors

| Connector | Meaning | Notes | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| けど | but, though | Casual; softens the tone. Often left hanging at the end of a polite sentence: *すみません、ちょっといそがしいけど…* | 日本語はむずかしいけど、おもしろいです。<br>(Japanese is difficult, but interesting.) |
| が | but | More formal / polite than けど | 雨がふっていますが、行きます。<br>(It's raining, but I'll go.) |
| でも | but, however | Starts a **new** sentence; casual | にくがすきです。でも、さかなはきらいです。<br>(I like meat. But I dislike fish.) |
| から | because, so | Neutral, everyday reason; attaches mid-sentence | あついから、まどをあけます。<br>(Because it's hot, I'll open the window.) |
| ので | because, so | Softer / politer than から | ねつがあるので、学校を休みます。<br>(I have a fever, so I'll stay home.) |
| だから | so, therefore | Like から but starts the **next** sentence | あついです。だから、まどをあけました。<br>(It's hot. So I opened the window.) |
| それで | so, therefore | Cause-and-result link between sentences (similar to だから) | あめでした。それで、でかけませんでした。<br>(It was raining. So I didn't go out.) |
| そして | and, and then | Neutral; lists or sequences events | 本を読みました。そして、ねました。<br>(I read a book. And then I slept.) |
| それから | and then, after that | Neutral; next step in a sequence | 学校へ行きました。それから、ともだちに会いました。<br>(I went to school. After that, I met a friend.) |
| それに | and also, in addition | Adds extra info; slightly above N5 but useful | そのみせはやすいです。それに、べんりです。<br>(That shop is cheap. And also, convenient.) |
| とき (時) | when | Links events to a time | 子どものとき、よくあそびました。<br>(When I was a child, I often played.) |

## What to prioritise

Master **けど**, **から**, **ので**, **とき** plus one or two of the simple "and / so" connectors (**そして**, **それから**, **だから**) and you'll be solid for N5.
